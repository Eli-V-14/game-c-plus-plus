How to make project work:
- Using CLion, click the pancakes in the top left
- Hovering over 'View', find the 'Tool Window' and click it
- After clicking it, find 'Vcpkg' towards the bottom of the list and click it
- Once Vcpkg and its dependencies have been downloaded, you will find the ICON for it at the bottom left with two stacked rectangles
- After clicking on the Vcpkg ICON, locate the '+', name it, and make sure to click the checkbox for 'Add vcpkg integration into existing CMake profiles' and 'Debug(uses 'vcpkg')'
